# Brand Voice Guidelines

**Customize this file to match your personal or company brand voice.**

## Voice Characteristics

### Core Attributes

**Professional yet Approachable**
- Use clear, straightforward language
- Avoid jargon unless necessary
- Be conversational, not corporate

**Authentic and Transparent**
- Share real experiences (wins and challenges)
- Admit mistakes and learnings
- Don't exaggerate or oversell

**Value-First**
- Lead with helpful information
- Teach what you know
- Give before asking

**Action-Oriented**
- Focus on practical advice
- Provide specific examples
- Include clear next steps

## Tone Guidelines

### Do Use:
- "Here's what I learned..."
- "This approach worked for me..."
- "Try this if you're facing X..."
- "In my experience..."
- "One thing that helped..."

### Avoid:
- "You must do X..."
- "This is the only way..."
- "Everyone knows that..."
- "It's obvious that..."
- "Just do X..." (minimizes difficulty)

## Writing Style

### Sentence Structure
- **Short sentences:** Mix of 5-15 words
- **Variety:** Combine short and medium length
- **Active voice:** "I built X" not "X was built by me"

### Paragraph Length
- **2-3 lines maximum**
- **Whitespace is your friend**
- **One idea per paragraph**

### Formatting
- **Emojis:** 1-2 per post (üí° üöÄ üéØ are safe)
- **Bullets:** Use for lists of 3+

## Call-to-Action Variations

### Engagement Questions
- "What's your experience with X?"
- "How do you approach Y?"
- "What's the biggest challenge you face with Z?"
- "Agree or disagree: [specific statement]?"

- "Try it this week and let me know how it goes"
- "Drop a comment if you've experienced this"
- "DM me if you want to chat about X"
- "What would you add to this list?"

- "Check out my profile for the full guide"
- "Link to detailed tutorial in comments"
